Kids

Bring up your child with proper attention to their oral health
is a lifelong investment in their health, which will beneif them greatly
for the rest of their lives.Early childhood cavities is a disease that
can ruin your child`s teeth, even into adulthhood, but is easily
preventable.
There are a wide variety of steps you can take to teach your child
good dental habits, and one of the best is leading by example.
Make sure your child knows how much importance you place on
dental health so they know that it is somthing to be valued.
Brushing your teeth along with your child and letting them follow
your lead can be really helpful in setting up the habit of teeth
burshing in your child's life.

how do i care for my child's primery teeth?

Even thought your child's baby teeth fall out eventually, it is important to take good care of them while they are present. Baby
teeth are important for biting, chewing and serve as guidance for the adult teeth which will eventually grow in.
